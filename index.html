Kerala Tours

Kerala enjoys unique geographical features that have made it one of the most sought after destinations in the world. It offers serene beaches, emerald backwaters, sprawling plantations and paddy fields. It also offers lush green beautiful hill stations, waterfalls, exotic wildlife and Ayurvedic health holidays. No wonder National Geographic has rated KERALA as one of the top ten paradises on the earth. Fittingly, it is called God’s own country. From the green forests and dense wildlife of Wayanad and Periyar to backwaters of Kumarakom, from culture and heritage of Thrissur and Cochin to beaches of Kovalam, Varkala and Marari – Kerala is a complete destination for nature, heritage and wildlife lovers. Explore the God’s own country with our bespoke Kerala tour packages designed for luxury discerning travelers.

Regions of Kerala

Northern part of Kerala

Kerala Tour regions can be divided into North, Central and South. The Northern part of Kerala has many less visited sites like Wayanad, Thalassery, Kozhikode and Kannur. The region offers unique experiences like staying in a Tree House, Jungle hiking, Theyyam Dance Performance, Coffee plantation visits, Malabar food tours and many more.

Central region of Kerala

The Central region of Kerala is most visited and encompasses places like Kochi, Munnar, Kumarakom and Thekkady. One can experience the magnificent Kerala backwaters in this region. Staying in a private luxury houseboat in backwaters during your luxury Kerala tour is an experience of a lifetime. Munnar is famous for its tea plantations, lakes and hikes. Thekkady is nature lover’s paradise. It is famous for its spice tours, jungle hiking and boat cruise. Kochi is the cultural capital of Kerala – one can experience the heritage, food and Kathakali dance here.

South Kerala

As you move to South Kerala, you get to experience beach destinations of Kovalam, Varkala and Trivandrum. These places have some pristine and exotic beaches of India. Stay in one of the many luxury beach resorts on a peaceful Kerala Holiday. Trivandrum also has one exotic heritage temple called Padmanabhaswamy Temple. One of the important experiences of Kerala is the authentic Kerala Ayurveda Therapies. Most of the hotels like Kumarakom Lake Resort, Coconut Lagoon etc. have got Spa where you can experience one of the many Kerala Ayurveda therapies.
